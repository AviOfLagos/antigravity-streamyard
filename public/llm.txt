# Zerocast - The AI-Powered Live Streaming Studio

Zerocast is a browser-based live streaming studio designed to be the smart alternative to platforms like StreamYard and Restream. It features a native AI Co-Host that acts as your production team.

## Core Features
- **AI Co-Host & Automation**: Train the AI on your voice, and it will moderate your multi-platform chat, welcome guests, and automatically handle scene switching.
- **Multistreaming**: Go live on YouTube, Twitch, Kick, TikTok, and custom RTMP destinations simultaneously.
- **Professional Studio**: Browser-based WebRTC studio with 5 layout presets, backstage management, and high-quality audio processing (echo cancellation, noise suppression).
- **Guest Management**: Frictionless join links, waiting rooms, and device preview.

## Architecture
Built on Next.js 15 (App Router), React 19, LiveKit (WebRTC/SFU), Neon Postgres, Upstash Redis, and Tailwind CSS v4.
